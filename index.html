Welcome to My PIVX Wallet!

Be your own Bank!

MyPIVXWallet has no custody over your funds. You are in full ownership of your keys and your PIV. With this wallet, you can use PIVX across the web, on-the-go, with full safety of your funds in your very own protection. The wallet has many customization options, can be downloaded and ran locally, has a dedicated offline mode, and can be cloned directly from GitHub!

Universal and Portable

With our built-in tools, you can generate cryptographically-secure addresses with the power of your browser and hardware. You can generate 'random' addresses or you can generate vanity addresses with your own custom prefixes. In addition, all keys are entirely universal and can be imported/exported directly to and from PIVX Core to this web wallet.

Don't trust, verify!

MyPIVXWallet is completely open-source, available on the PIVX Labs github - a community-ran organization for PIVX-oriented OSS projects, don't trust us? Good! Download the source code, kill your wifi, lock yourself in a bunker and the wallet will still function in Offline mode, with no dependency on the internet for barebones functionality, use 'Manual Transactions' to manually form raw transactions while offline, and broadcast them at your own pace, through your own node.




WARNING: if you don't save your private key you will lose access to your funds!
